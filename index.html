We Love Each Other

Our Story

  • How It All Began

    2005

    It began in elementary school, where two young lives first intersected—unaware of the journey that time would weave between them. From the start, she noticed him, though her feelings remained unspoken.

  • Quiet and Fading Feelings

    2011

    Years later, school brought them back into each other’s orbits. The spark was still there, subtle but present, yet she kept her distance, her emotions hidden behind quiet glances and missed moments. Circumstances pulled them in different directions, and slowly, they lost touch. The connection dimmed, and what once felt possible faded into memory.

  • A New Beginning

    2018

    Time passed, life moved on—but then, unexpectedly, their paths crossed again. This time, it was his heart that opened first. After so many years apart, they reconnected, and something finally clicked. Conversations flowed effortlessly, laughter came easily, and shared passions—movies, music, and even the thrill of discovering new foods—drew them closer than ever before.

  • From Us to Always

    Now – Forever

    Six years have passed since that reconnection, filled with countless memories, endless laughter, and unwavering love. Together, they’ve built something lasting—something real. And now, they’re ready to begin the next chapter—the one that goes beyond "together" and into "forever."
